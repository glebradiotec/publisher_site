info About the Journal

"Antennas" is a peer-reviewed scientific journal dedicated to the dissemination of new results in the theory and practice of antenna systems. The journal covers a wide range of topics including advanced antenna theory, design methodologies, wave propagation analysis, and electromagnetic compatibility. We aim to bridge the gap between theoretical research and practical engineering applications in telecommunications, radar systems, and remote sensing.
